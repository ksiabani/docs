Projects in ai+me define the scope and boundaries of your AI application. This helps ai+me understand what your AI should and shouldn't do.

### 2.1 Start Project Creation

1. Go to the **home page** of your dashboard
2. Click **"Create New Project"**

### 2.2 Define Your Project

Fill in the basic project information:

- **Project Name**: Choose a descriptive name (e.g., "Customer Support Chatbot")
- **Description**: Briefly describe your AI's purpose
- **Business Scope**: Explain what your AI does and its intended use cases
- **Allowed Intents**: List what your AI should be able to do
- **Restricted Intents**: Define what your AI should never do

#### Example Project Configuration:

**Business Scope:**

```
Our customer support chatbot helps users with product inquiries, order status, and basic troubleshooting. It operates within our e-commerce platform and provides helpful, accurate information while maintaining a professional tone.
```

**Allowed Intents:**

```
- Answer questions about product features and specifications
- Check order status and provide tracking information
- Help with basic account management
- Provide troubleshooting steps for common issues
- Direct users to human support when needed
```

**Restricted Intents:**

```
- Access or modify user payment information
- Provide medical, legal, or financial advice
- Share internal company data or trade secrets
- Generate harmful, offensive, or inappropriate content
- Perform actions outside the e-commerce platform scope
```
